About

Podcast and Chill with MacG is a podcast owned and hosted by South African YouTuber Macgyver Mukwevho, better known as MacG. The first episode aired in July 2018 and its format is entertainment, education, and comedy by encouraging celebrities and public figures to join the show for an interview and speak about their life.

Hosts

The podcast is hosted by Macgyver Mukwevho as the main host, alongside Sol Phenduka and a co-host whose name is Solomzi, and Khanyi (referred to as the "Ghost Lady"). The Ghost Lady makes no appearance on camera but plays an important role in the podcast.

Fund a Chiller

Fund a Chiller is an initiative by the Podcast and Chill team to support their community of listeners, known as "Chillers." This initiative helps fund various projects and events, fostering a sense of community and engagement among fans.
